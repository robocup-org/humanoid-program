RoboCup Humanoid Robot Program

Update for 2026

Opportunities for Teams to Access Cutting-Edge Humanoid Robots

Program Overview

The RoboCup Federation has partnered with top humanoid robot manufacturers (Booster, Fourier, Unitree and High Torque) to offer exclusive access to cutting-edge humanoid robots at discounted rates for participating teams. This initiative aims to promote participation in RoboCup 2026, which will take place in Incheon, South Korea.

General Conditions for All Robots

Participation:

Teams that purchased any of the humanoid robots under this agreement need to participate in the RoboCup International event or major Super-Regional or official RoboCup Regional Competitions.

Selection:

Existing RoboCup teams (including Soccer Simulation teams) can directly reach out to the vendor.

New RoboCup teams will need to apply to this program (see Apply button at the bottom of this page).

Notice:

League rules and requirements apply!

    Other Conditions of the Agreement

    Company's Support:

    If any defect is detected in any robot purchased from The Company (whether it is the Booster T1, Fourier GR-1, Unitree G1, or any other model covered under this agreement), The Company agrees to provide repair support, provided that the defect is not due to the team's willful misconduct or negligence. The conditions for the repair support are outlined below:

    • Online support will be provided to the team if they choose to repair the defect themselves. The Company will supply the necessary parts for the repair free of charge.
    • If the team chooses to send the robot to The Company for repair, the repair will be conducted free of charge.
    • Shipping costs for repairs will be borne by the team. However, if the defect is reported within six months from the completion of delivery, The Company will cover the shipping cost.
    • If a defect occurs during the RoboCup competition period at the competition venue, The Company will provide free repair support, unless the defect is due to the buyer’s willful misconduct or negligence.
